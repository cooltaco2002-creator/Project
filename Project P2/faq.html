Frequently Asked Questions

  1. What is Fish Finder?

    A prototype front-end to collect and display fish observations; later it will be connected to Python analysis and a database.

  2. How can I submit observations?

    Use the Submit form (link in navigation). You may attach images and provide location information.

  3. Who can use the data?

    Data is for project use; access policies will be defined when the backend is added.
